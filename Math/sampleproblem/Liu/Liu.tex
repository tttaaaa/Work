\begin{exs}
  Determine the sheaf associated to the presheaf $A_X$ of Example 2.4. Such
a sheaf is called a constant sheaf. Show that $A_X$ is a sheaf if and only if every non-empty open subset of $X$ is connected.
\end{exs}

\begin{exs}
  Let $\mathcal{F}$ be a sheaf on $X$. Let $s,t \in \mathcal{F}(X)$.
  Show that the set of $x \in X$ such that $s_x = t_x$ is open in X.
\end{exs}
\begin{proof}
  germの定義から$s_x = t_x$となる場合，ある開集合$U_x$が存在し，
  $\rho_{U_x}(s)=\rho_{U_x}(t)$となる．
  この時,任意の$y \in U_x$に対し，$s_y=t_y$となる．
  そのため，the set of $x \in X$ such that $s_x = t_x$は
  $\cup_x U_x$となり，開集合になる．
\end{proof}
